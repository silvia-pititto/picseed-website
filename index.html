About Us

Picseed is founded on a mutual passion for art and design. Our team is small but mighty, made up of Irish and Italian muscle. We, Silvia Pititto and Lisa Reburn met during our MA in Creative Digital Media at DIT and have joined forces to bring you Picseed. Our backgrounds comprise of design and programming. We are committed to experimenting with the latest state-of-art technology and design solutions in order to deliver a unique innovative product.

We empathise with people who have a thirst to get a project off the ground but struggle to find inspiration. We understand how time consuming it is to find reputable and interesting content. We use providers such as Behance and the best Tumblr curated art blogs to inspire you to stay creative.
